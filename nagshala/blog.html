Yoga Life

NAGASHREE INTRODUCTION

26 FEB 2022

Being an IT professional for three years and an avid practitioner of Yoga, I thought that I could somehow manage both my profession and passion. But as days went on, I realized what my soul was actually craving for! And then it occurred to me that, the pursuit of happiness is not in just working for hours together to pay bills and just achieve materialistic success. Though I knew all this theoretically, after a brief period working in the corporate field, I realized on a deeper level that life is much more than just chasing worldly success.

After the realization, there was a paradigm shift in my thought process. The monotonous lifestyle of the corporate world made me realize that greed and obsession for money is a vicious cycle that we get ourselves into. This eventually led to deeper level changes in the way I perceived situations and life in general.

As an avid practitioner of Yoga since my childhood, I have explored many places and have met many people who are in genuine need of help. So, when I started my profession and as my career progressed, I deeply thought about how I could bring about a change in society and lead a life that’s not just beneficial to me but also to all the people around me. I felt the urge to reach out to people who are in genuine need of help. And this prompted me to quit my IT profession and choose a platform where I could share my passion with everybody and also contribute to society.

The success of Yoga does not lie in the ability to perform postures but in how it positively changes the way we live our life and our relationships.

T.K.V. Desikachar

The sole reason why I chose this platform is to spread positivity in the form of Yoga, Art, Dance and Music and to show how one can benefit from all the above art forms to replenish their soul and rejuvenate their Mind, Body and Spirit.

Here, I will be sharing content, articles and posts related to General wellness with practical tools and techniques, that mainly include Yoga that covers the mental, emotional and physical aspects of our lives.

There have been many other holistic and general wellness websites and platforms, but what makes this website unique is the way messages or deeper truths of life are conveyed in the form of art and how easily one can incorporate these beautiful truths in life.

Exchanging thoughts, ideas and life-changing habits will always result in building a healthy society, isn’t it? If we start working on ourselves and strive to become a better person every day, that is the greatest gift that we could give to ourselves and society.

So, hope you all find the content relatable and interesting!

Thank you!
